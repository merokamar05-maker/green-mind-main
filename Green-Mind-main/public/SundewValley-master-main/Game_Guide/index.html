How to Play

Welcome to Sundew Valley! Here are the basic controls and mechanics to get you started on your farm.

Movement

Use W A S D or the Arrow Keys (↑, ↓, ←, →) to move your character around the map.

Water Crops

Press Q while equipped with the watering can to water your crops and dirt.

Dig Soil

Press E while using the hoe to dig up the soil and prepare it for planting.

Plant Seeds

Left Click on wet soil while holding seeds in your active bar to plant them.

Harvesting

Press C when standing near fully grown crops to harvest them.

Inventory Management

Press I to open Inventory. While open, press Alt + Right Click to move items to your hotbar.

Sleeping

Approach your bed and Left Click to sleep, save your progress, and skip to the next day.

NPC Interaction

Approach an NPC and Left Click to talk or trade with them for unique items.
